My initial plan after graduating undergrad was to work in the health care sector, however I soon came to realize through my experiences that I did not enjoy the feild as much as I originally thought. My interest for coding/software engineering came about after taking with some of my freinds who were in the tech industry. Although I had alway had a passion for technology, I wasn't sure what career in it meant. After doing some self learning in coding, I decided to apply for a Master's program at the University of Pennsylvania in Computer and Information Technology. As this is my last semester in the program, I can confidently say that it was the right choice for me. Throught out the Master's program, I gained experience working with multiple technologies and built a strong Computer Science foundatation. This provided me with the ability to efficiently and quickly adapt to any work environment and learn new tools/technologies.
