Learn More About Me!

I am a Master's student in Computer Science at the University of Pennsylvania. In addition to my studies, I serve as a tutor and academic coach for the Introduction to Software Development course in the CIT program. I earned my Bachelor's degree in Biomedical Sciences with a minor in Biochemistry from Arizona State University in 2021. Initially, I pursued a career in healthcare, but after gaining exposure to the tech industry through friends, I discovered my passion for coding and software engineering. I self-studied and eventually applied for a Master's program at the University of Pennsylvania in Computer and Information Technology. As I near the end of the program, I can confidently say it was the right decision for me. Through this program, I have acquired hands-on experience with various technologies and a strong foundation in computer science, which has equipped me to effectively adapt to new work environments and technologies.
